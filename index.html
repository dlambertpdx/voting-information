Register to Vote:

  • Register to vote online using Oregon Secretary of State's My Vote. An Oregon driver's license or state ID card issued by the Oregon DMV is required.

Sorry, you're not eligible to register to vote.

You may register to vote if:

  • You are an Oregon resident.
  • You are a U.S. Citizen.
  • You are at least 17 years of age (If you are 17 years of age, you will not receive a ballot until an election occurs on or after your 18th birthday).
